1st Year: The Foundations

I began my Data Science journey on November 25, 2023. This transition was not without challenges, particularly due to the accumulated delay. Nevertheless, through my commitment, I was able to make up for this lag. This year allowed me to discover the richness of the discipline, through courses combining theory (Math, Stats) and practice (Dev, Dataviz). I admit I could have gotten more out of this year if I had been more diligent from the start, but this realization led me to seriously reflect on my commitment.

Beyond the academic aspect, this year was also an important step in my personal growth. I learned to better manage my time and organize myself more autonomously.

This year of specialization (VCOD Path) was demanding. I worked hard to validate the statistics module, essential to the program. I developed advanced skills in Business Intelligence and web development applied to data.

In parallel, my half-marathon challenge taught me discipline and long-term effort management, qualities I now apply to my work.
