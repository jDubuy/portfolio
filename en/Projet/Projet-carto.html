Project: Cartography

This project aimed to have us apply our cartography skills using QGIS software.

Project Objectives

We had to answer several questions about locating a new McDonald's restaurant, based on analyses performed with QGIS. The maps produced, as well as our answers, had to be submitted as a Word document.

Conclusion

This project allowed us to consolidate our mastery of QGIS software while developing our ability to produce readable and informative maps in a decision-support context.
