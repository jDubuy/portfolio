Project: Controversy

This project aimed to have us write a report on a controversial topic.

Project Objectives

We had to choose a controversial topic, analyze the arguments of different parties and the values they defended. Our group chose the theme of video surveillance in France and wrote a report of about 10 pages presenting our analysis of this controversy.

Conclusion

This project allowed us to develop our information research and discourse analysis skills.
