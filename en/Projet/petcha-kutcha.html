Project: Pecha Kucha

This project aimed to have us conduct research on a personality as part of a national competition between students.

Project Objectives

We had to choose a person who had been deprived of freedoms and who fought to reclaim them. We then had to write a first-person narrative tracing their journey, explain our choice, and reflect on what we ourselves would be willing to do to defend our freedoms. For my part, I chose Captain Paul Watson.

We also had to create an oral presentation of the Pecha Kucha type on this person.

Conclusion

This project improved my research, storytelling, and public speaking skills. It also led me to reflect on fundamental personal values such as freedom and commitment.
