Project: Interactive Dashboard Creation

This project was completed in a team of two in November 2025. The objective was to create an interactive dashboard using QlikSense to visualize and analyze complex data in an intuitive way, focusing on the influence of alcohol on the lifestyle habits of French people.

Project Objectives

We had to design and set up relevant charts and key performance indicators (KPIs) to best represent the data. The main objective was to make the analysis of this issue clear and accessible to everyone through appropriate data visualizations.

Execution & Organization

To carry out this work, we proceeded in stages:

  • Development and optimization of data loading scripts (ETL).
  • Selection and design of the most suitable visualizations to represent the data in a clear and engaging way.
  • Continuous collaboration within the pair to exchange ideas and refine the presentation of indicators.

Challenges Faced

One of the main challenges was ensuring that the data was correctly integrated and that the visualizations were both informative and aesthetic. In addition, learning some of QlikSense's advanced features required an adaptation period. We overcame these challenges through smooth communication and effective division of tasks.

Conclusion & Skills Acquired

This project allowed me to develop my skills in data visualization with QlikSense as well as my teamwork capabilities. I reinforced my understanding of best practices in interactive dashboard design and visualization layout.
