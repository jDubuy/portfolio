Project: JavaScript Website - Airbnb

This project was completed in a group of four as part of our training in the VCOD specialization. It aimed to practice our JavaScript development skills and deploy a website via GitHub Pages.

Project Objectives

We had to design a website on the theme of Airbnb accommodations in the ÃŽle-de-France region. Several criteria had to be met, including the exclusive use of the Plotly library for creating charts. The website had to include:

  • A dashboard page containing several global indicators;
  • A page with choropleth maps;
  • A page with small multiples type charts.

Implementation

To organize our work, we distributed the pages among team members: each was assigned a specific page, and two of us collaborated on the dashboard. Once development was complete, we integrated all parts into a common website. To innovate in our presentation, we also integrated our PowerPoint presentation directly into the site.

Conclusion

This project allowed us to consolidate our JavaScript, data visualization, and team project management skills. Despite a very tight deadline, we managed to deliver a functional, well-structured, and interactive website. This time constraint taught us to better prioritize tasks and work efficiently under pressure.
