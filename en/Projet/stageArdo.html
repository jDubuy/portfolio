Internship: Database Redesign and Management Tools

I completed this 10-week internship within the Ardo group (ALS site in Saint-Sever), in the packaging department. My main objective was to improve the reliability and fluidity of internal production files by automating their management through custom tools.

Project Objectives

  • Redesign the "product bible" into a single, reliable and structured database
  • Automate data synchronization between different files in use
  • Standardize production times to improve planning
  • Create a CRP tool for annual planning

Methodology

I used Python (pandas, openpyxl, etc.) to automate file analysis, cleaning, and merging. Then I integrated macros into Excel to automate weekly scheduling (MRP) and synchronize product information. I also implemented statistical standardization of production rates.

Results Achieved

  • A production file automatically synchronized to the product bible
  • A resettable weekly schedule, more reliable and simple to use
  • An automatic MRP allowing anticipation of consumable shortages
  • A custom CRP tool for planning annual workload

Conclusion

This internship allowed me to apply my data science skills in a concrete context, working on useful tools immediately exploitable by the company. It also made me aware of industrial constraints and the importance of ergonomics in decision-support tools.
