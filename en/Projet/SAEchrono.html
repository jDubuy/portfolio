Project: SAE - Time Series Forecasting

This SAE aimed to help us practice our skills in statistics and hypothesis testing.

Project Objectives

We had to analyze and attempt to predict the volume of imports (in millions of dollars) for a country of our choice. For my part, I worked on imports to Australia. The final objective was to submit a report detailing our analyses and the forecasting models used.

Conclusion

This project allowed me to strengthen my skills in time series forecasting using different statistical models, as well as improve my writing abilities in an analytical context.
