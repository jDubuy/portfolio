Project: Database SID Modeling

This project, completed in a duo, aimed to practice our skills in database design and implementation.

Project Objectives

  • Apply our knowledge in database programming.
  • Deepen our thinking on modeling and designing relational schemas.

Context

The project focused on managing data for a dental practice, including information about patients, practitioners, and treatments performed.

Conceptual Data Model (CDM)

The CDM we designed allowed us to visualize the interactions between main entities: Dentist, Patient, Consultation, and Treatment.

Application

We then translated this CDM into a functional database, populated and tested using SQL queries to validate its consistency.

Conclusion

This project, though relatively simple, allowed us to effectively review the fundamentals of data modeling and apply them in a real context. It also strengthened our ability to work smoothly in a duo.
