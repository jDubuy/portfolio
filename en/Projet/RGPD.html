Project: GDPR Compliance

This project aimed to apply standards and rules related to GDPR.

Project Objectives

We had to advise a fictional company on compliance with GDPR requirements and guide them on actions to take to comply with regulations.

Conclusion

This project helped us better understand the issues related to personal data protection and the obligations imposed by GDPR. By advising a fictional company, we learned to identify non-compliance points and propose concrete solutions to ensure regulatory compliance. This experience also made us aware of the importance of integrating privacy protection into all digital processes of an organization.
